Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026 • Homefront v1.0.0

🔒 Homefront stores all your data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers.

This privacy policy explains how Homefront handles your information. Because everything stays on your device, there is very little to explain — but we want to be completely transparent.

What data does Homefront store?

Homefront stores the following in your browser’s localStorage — on your device only:

  • Your first name (used for the home screen greeting)
  • Home profile settings (pets, hard water, allergies, household size, etc.)
  • Appliance details including model and serial numbers, maintenance schedules, and completion history
  • Warranty information you enter
  • Contractor contacts you save
  • Seasonal checklist progress
  • Home Systems task completions
  • App preferences (dark mode, notifications)

None of this data ever leaves your device unless you export it using the backup feature.

Do you collect any data?

No. Homefront does not collect analytics, use tracking cookies, send your data to any server, create user accounts, or require an internet connection to function.

Internet connection

Homefront loads fonts from Google Fonts on first launch. After that it works fully offline. No personal data is sent during this request — it is a standard font file download.

Data export and backup

You can export your data at any time from Settings. This creates a file saved to your device that you control. You can import it to restore your data or move it to another device.

We recommend exporting a backup periodically — clearing your browser storage will delete all app data.

Children’s privacy

Homefront is designed for homeowners and adults. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. Because we collect no data at all, there is no special handling required.

Changes to this policy

If we add features that change how data is handled, we will update this policy and note the date at the top. We will never begin collecting personal data without clear notice.
